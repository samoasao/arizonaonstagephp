P Arizona Onstage Productions Closer Than Ever

PRESENTS




CLOSER THAN EVER, a musical revue in two acts,
with words by Richard Maltby, Jr. and music by David Shire.
Featuring Liz Cracchiolo, Amy Erbe, Brian Levario and Kit Runge
Directed by Kevin Johnson with Elliot Jones as music director
and Dylan DeRobertis on upright bass/cello
Production Stage Manager, Adam Norrish
Lighting, Set and Sound design by Zachary Ciaburri
Choreography by Samantha Cormier
These songs celebrate the gamut of human relationships, from birth to death, from love to hate, from together to separate, from the present to the future.
At the Cabaret Space, Temple of Music and Art, 330 S. Scott Ave.
Performance dates, August 1-18.
Preview, August 1, 8pm.
Regular Friday and Saturday performances, 8pm.
Sunday matinees, 3pm.

No performance on August 16th!
