Knuffle Bunny


Based on the Caldecott Honor Award winning children's picture book, script and lyrics by Mo Willems, music by Michael Silversher, KNUFFLE BUNNY - A CAUTIONARY MUSICAL follows Trixie, a baby just learning to talk and her journey to find her beloved stuffed bunny, lost in the washer/dryer. After the show, enjoy an Eegees and take part in a bubble party with the cast!

Directed by Kevin Johnson and Samantha Cormier
Choreography by Samantha Cormier
Maria Gallardo, Stage Manager
Lighting Design by Don Fox
Featuring Brian Levario, Chezale Rodriguez-Wells and Autumn Ruhe
with dancer/puppeteers Cisiany Olivar and Carrie Silverman
