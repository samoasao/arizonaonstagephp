PRESENTS



Directed by Terry Erbe
Veronica Haakonsen, Stage Manager
Set Design by Mike Boyd

Featuring Amy Erbe
Carrie Hill
Lori Hunt
Robert Anthony Peters
Carley Preston
and Debbie Runge
By the creator and producer of "True Blood" and "Six Feet Under," this irreverent and funny look at complex friendships and the power of identical dressing takes place at a wedding reception in Knoxville, Tennessee. The women are hiding out in an upstairs bedroom, each having her own reasons to avoid the proceedings going on below.

For mature audiences.

At the Cabaret Space, Temple of Music and Art, 330 S. Scott Ave.
Playing January 10-27, 2013.
