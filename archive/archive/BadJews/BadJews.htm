BAD JEWS

Rated R for extreme profanity!


By Joshua Harmon

Directed by Kevin Johnson

Featuring Shira Maas, Beth May
Jeremy Vega and Luka Vonier

Production Stage Manager, Bill Bethel
Set Designer, Mark Tremblay


Joshua Harmon's critically acclaimed play asks questions about what you choose to believe, when you're chosen and tells the story of Daphna Feygenbaum, a "Real Jew" with an Israeli boyfriend. When Daphna's cousin Liam brings home his shiksa girlfriend Melody and declares ownership of their grandfather's Chai necklace, a vicious and hilarious brawl over family, faith and legacy ensues.
