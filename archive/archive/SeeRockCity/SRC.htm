See Rock City & Other Destinations, by Brad Alexander and Adam Mathias, which features urban tales set to original songs and dialogue, won the 2011 Drama Desk award for best book. Featured singers include Amy DeHaven, Nick Gallardo, Miranda Sloan, Brian Levario, Rob Roberts, Jolene Lucas, Kevin Vidal, Justin Moore and Jon Holmes. Directed by Megan Tenney, Music Direction, Daniel Tenney, Costumes by Nora Petty. Plays November 3-20 at the Cabaret Space, Temple of Music and Art. Performances are November 3, 4, 5, 11, 12, 18 and 19 at 7:30pm. Sunday matinees, November 6, 13 and 20 at 3:00pm.

Tickets for See Rock City are $22.50 for students/seniors and $25.00 general admission (plus a small service charge). Preview on November 3 is $15.00.
