Arizona Onstage Productions presents:



In this must see musical, a 3rd grader tries for the leading role in a play her class is putting on, and insures comedy at every step of the journey. This musical spoofs leading Broadway shows and movies while telling a touching story.

This musical has been awarded an Outer Critic's Circle Award for best Off-Broadway musical, and was performed in from January 13-30, 2005 at Zuzi's Little Theatre, Fifth and University.

It featured David Olsen, Stephanie Sikes, Jalyn Wheatley, Liz Cracchiolo-Redman, Shayna Vercillo, Sarah Spiegelman and Diane Thomas. Directed by Kevin Johnson with musical direction by Chris Wingert, choreography by Stacy Johnson and lighting design by Eric Michael.

This musical has been awarded an Outer Critic's Circle Award for best Off-Broadway musical and a Drama Desk Award for Best Music and Lyrics, and will be performed in January of 2005.
