Devil Boys from Beyond

The 2009 NY Fringe Fest Smash Hit!
AOP's first late-night show (taking the stage after See Rock City), Devil Boys from Beyond!

Summer of 1957 is a scorcher and something strange is going on in the swamps of Lizard Lick, Florida. With rumors of missing people and giant spaceships running rampant, star NYC reporter Mattie Van Bur en races down in search of her next Pulitzer, her booze-hound ex-husband, Gregory Graham in tow, and her arch-enemy, Lucinda Marsh hot on her heels to scoop her story. Flying saucers! Backstabbing bitches! Muscle hunks and men in pumps! INVASION OF THE BODY SNATCHERS meets HIS GIRL FRIDAY in this outrageously insane comedy by Buddy Thomas Rated R for Rude!

Directed by Fred Rodriguez. With Ina Shivack, Steve McKee, T. Loving, Eric Taylor, Andrew Baughman, Nick Gallardo,
Brian Levario and Rob Roberts

Plays November 3-20 at the Cabaret Space, Temple of Music and Art. Performances are November 3, 4, 5, 11, 12, 18 and 19 at 10pm. Sunday matinees, November 6 and 13 at 6:30pm and November 20 at 6:00pm. Tickets are $10 for the preview and $15.00 otherwise (plus a small service charge)
. On November 20, the last performance is at 6pm.
