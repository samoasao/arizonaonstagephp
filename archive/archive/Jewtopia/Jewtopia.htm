Bryan Fogel and Sam Wolfson's fierce comedy plays in Tucson for the first time.


JEWTOPIA explores Judaism through warped eyes and battles stereotypes of the faith with comedy and a very huge heart. The original production of JEWTOPIA is the longest running non-musical in Los Angeles theater history. It ran for over 2 years to sold-out houses. The New York production ran for over 3 successful years.
